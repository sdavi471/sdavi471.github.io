About Me

Welcome to The Chaos Coordinator! I'm glad you're here. I'm Stephanie - wife to my amazing husband, Caleb, and stay-at-home momma to my four little BFFs. 

Rooted in Christian values, this blog offers encouragement, practical tips, and real-life wisdom for managing the everyday whirlwind of motherhood.

Whether you came looking for a children's activity idea or an easy weeknight recipe, I hope you leave feeling capable and encouraged through the hope of the Gospel. My goal is for you to see that peace and joy can truly be found in the chaos and the mundane.
